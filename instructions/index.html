Your Guide to
"Home Cooking Made Easy"
with PEASY

  1. “Plan” Your Meals: Start by drawing your Meal Cards for the week. This is the fun part—let chance plan what's for dinner (or lunch)!
  2. Create Your Shopping List: Use the page numbers on your cards to find the recipes in the PEASY Recipe Book. Check what you already have, then fill out your shopping list with just what you need for the week’s grocery trip.
  3. Organize Your Week: Assign each meal to a day of the week.
  4. Enjoy Cooking: On cooking day, simply open the recipe book to the correct page and cook away!
